This Project studies the effect of the Centrality of capital cities in the political stability of countries. This work is an adaptation of the participation of Houcemeddine Turki to Toshiba-NSTA ExploraVision (2011, with Bassel Belhadj and Achraf Messaoud), Google Science Fair (2012 and 2013), the National Qualifiers of the Intel International Science and Engineering Fair (2012), and the Intenational Environmental Project Olympiad (2012, with Bassel Belhadj). This Project is also an adaptation of the Capstone Project of Houcemeddine Turki for the Certificate in Research Methodology delivered by Alexis Foundation (2013).
